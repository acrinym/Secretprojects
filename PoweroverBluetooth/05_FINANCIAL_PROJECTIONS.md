Base: 500K units × $20 avg = $10M (Year 3)

```

**Industrial IoT**:
```
Assumption: First deployments Year 3, scale Year 4-5
Projects: 5-10 facilities per year initially
Average project: $200K (sensors + infrastructure + installation)

Conservative: 5 projects × $150K = $0.75M (Year 3)
Base: 8 projects × $200K = $1.6M (Year 3)
Bull: 12 projects × $250K = $3M (Year 3)
```

**Subscription Services**:
```
Assumption: 10-20% of devices convert to premium tier

Attach Rate: 15% at $1.50/month average

Base Case Calculation (Year 3):
- Medical: 500K devices × 5% attach (enterprise service) = 25K subs × $2/mo = $600K/year
- Smart Home: 500K devices × 20% attach = 100K subs × $1/mo = $1.2M/year
- Total: $1.8M recurring revenue (Year 3)

**Certification & Standards**:
```
Assumption: Battery-Free Certified program launches Year 3
Certified Products: 20-50 products by Year 3, growing to 500+ by Year 5
Fees: $10K average certification + $5K annual renewal

Base Case: 30 products × $10K initial + 10 renewals × $5K = $350K (Year 3)
Year 5: 500 products × $10K initial + 200 renewals × $5K = $6M
```

## RISK FACTORS & MITIGATION

### Business Risks

**Market Adoption Risk** (Medium)
- Risk: Customers unwilling to adopt new technology
- Impact: Revenue shortfall, slower growth
- Mitigation: Start with clear pain points (medical recalls), build case studies

**Technology Risk** (Medium)
- Risk: Efficiency targets not achieved
- Impact: Limited addressable market, higher costs
- Mitigation: Multiple parallel approaches (6 breakthrough mechanisms)

**Competitive Risk** (Medium-High)
- Risk: Large incumbent (Apple, Samsung) enters market
- Impact: Price pressure, market share loss
- Mitigation: Speed to market, IP moat, ecosystem lock-in, potential acquirer

**Regulatory Risk** (Medium)
- Risk: FDA/FCC delays or rejections
- Impact: Market entry delay, increased costs
- Mitigation: Start with favorable regulations (near-field), build political coalition

### Financial Risks

**Burn Rate Risk** (Low-Medium)
- Risk: Costs exceed projections
- Impact: Need to raise capital sooner/larger amounts
- Mitigation: Phased development, milestone-based spending, capital-efficient model

**Revenue Timing Risk** (Medium)
- Risk: Customers delay deployments
- Impact: Cash flow issues, miss projections
- Mitigation: Conservative revenue assumptions, maintain 12-18 month cash buffer

**Margin Pressure Risk** (Low)
- Risk: Competition forces price cuts
- Impact: Lower profitability
- Mitigation: High IP-driven margins harder to compete against, quality differentiation
