# Clear Haze — Risk & Regulatory Strategy

## FDA Compliance
Clear Haze is a dietary supplement, not a drug. Avoid disease claims. Stick to:
- “Supports cognitive clarity.”
- “Helps maintain memory.”
- “Supports healthy neuroinflammation response.”

## Label Requirements
- Supplement facts panel
- GRAS ingredients
- Allergen disclaimer
- GMP certification logo

## Legal Risks
- Claims scrutiny
- Ingredient sourcing verification
- Terpene transport laws (all food-grade, non-cannabis derived)

## Mitigation Strategies
- Third-party testing
- COA batch posting
- Legal review before launch
