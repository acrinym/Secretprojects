# Clear Haze — Brand Package

## Brand Essence
A cognitive firewall for cannabis users. Precision. Clarity. Control.

## Target Persona
The High-Functioning User: engineers, creatives, medical users, socialites who want the high *and* their mind intact.

## Brand Voice
- Premium
- Futuristic
- Clean
- Confident
- High-tech biohacker aesthetic

## Logo Concepts
1. Minimalist haze ring around a clean font.
2. Radiant lines implying “clarity breaking through fog.”
3. THC molecule outline dissolving into clean geometric pattern.

## Taglines
- “Get High. Remember Everything.”
- “Clarity Without Compromise.”
- “Your Mind, Upgraded.”

## Color Palette
- Midnight black (#00010D)
