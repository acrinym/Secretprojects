# Clear Haze — Marketing Campaign

## Campaign Theme
**“The Smart High.”**  
Biohack your cannabis experience.

## Influencer Targets
- Tech YouTubers (MKBHD-adjacent orbit)
- Biohackers (Ben Greenfield-type)
- Cannabis educators
- Neurochemistry podcasts
- TikTok creators showing “before/after fog”

## Content Types
- 30-second reels showing “fog → clarity”
- Split-screen “with vs. without Clear Haze”
- Testimonials from engineers, coders, artists
- Educational breakdown of COX-2 / astrocyte mechanism
- Paid podcast spots

## Launch Timeline
### Week 1–2
Influencer seeding, TikTok micro-influencers, reddit threads.

### Week 3–4
Paid IG + YT ads, SEO content blast, PR release.

### Week 5
Wholesale push to dispensaries.

### Week 6
Retargeting, subscription push, lookalike audiences.
