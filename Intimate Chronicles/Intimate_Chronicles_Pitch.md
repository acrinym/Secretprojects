## HOW IT WORKS (Simple Version)

### Setup:
1. Both partners create linked accounts (age verified, mutual consent)
2. Share relationship history (how you met, meaningful memories, inside jokes)
3. Each partner sets preferences privately (pacing, language style, boundaries)
4. System shows overlap and creates shared profile (both must approve)

### Generation:
1. Request a story (memory-based, fantasy scenario, or quick generate)
2. AI analyzes using 10-layer ethical reasoning engine
3. Generates personalized story incorporating:
   - Your actual memories together
   - Your stated preferences
   - Your relationship dynamic
   - Respecting ALL boundaries
4. Story delivered encrypted (only you two can read it)

### Feedback:
1. Both partners rate and provide feedback
2. System learns what works for YOUR relationship specifically
3. Improves over time based on YOUR preferences

**3. Relationship-Enhancing**

- Incorporates real memories
- Respects individual preferences
- Adapts to relationship dynamics
